From the School Education Department

GIC-Wajyula operates under the guidance of the State Education Department, committed to providing quality education to all sections of society. Our school follows the National Education Policy 2020 framework, focusing on holistic development of students.

The school has been recognized as a "Atal Utkrisht" by the Ministry of Education for its innovative teaching methods and excellent academic performance. We are proud to maintain a 100% track record of compliance with all government education standards and regulations.

Our infrastructure development projects are supported by the Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan, ensuring that students have access to modern facilities including smart classrooms, computer labs, and a well-stocked library.

About Us

Our History

Established in 1965, AUPSP GIC WAJULA has been a beacon of quality education in the region. Our school was founded with the vision to provide affordable, high-quality education to students from all backgrounds.

Over the years, we have grown from a small school with 50 students to a premier educational institution with over 1,200 students and 60 staff members.

Our alumni have gone on to excel in various fields including medicine, engineering, civil services, arts, and sports.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a stimulating learning environment that promotes academic excellence, creativity, and character development.

We aim to nurture students to become responsible citizens who contribute positively to society while upholding the values of integrity, respect, and compassion.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a center of excellence in education that empowers students to achieve their full potential and become leaders in their chosen fields.
